SUPPORT OUR CHILDREN

Grandma's Love, Inc. is committed to providing children in underserved communities with access to nutritious food and educational resources. Our programs, including Food Support Program, Book Vending Machines, and community engagement initiatives, aim to combat childhood hunger and promote literacy. Through your support, we can make a meaningful impact on the well-being and development of children in New York City.

![child writing with the help of an adult](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/41d000_efedb5f6f20d755329ac9d73a84bacbc.jpg/v1/fill/w_245,h_350,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/41d000_efedb5f6f20d755329ac9d73a84bacbc.jpg)

Join us at our upcoming events to make a difference in the lives of children. Your participation will help us raise awareness & support for our cause. Additionally, we recognize and celebrate outstanding contributions through our award programs, inspiring others to get involved in our mission.
