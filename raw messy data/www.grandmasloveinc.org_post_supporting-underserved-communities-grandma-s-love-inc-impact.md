# Supporting Underserved Communities: Grandma's Love Inc. Impact

- ![Writer: boardchair14](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/a/ACg8ocJaVPJVAPzWIafTmTdyZTM2eY6jcmOWRVbv40p3mq7XZzTYzsk=s96-c)
boardchair14
- Feb 4, 2025
- 2 min read

In the bustling streets of Brooklyn, New York, a beacon of hope shines bright for underserved communities. Founded in 2015, Grandma's Love Inc. is a non-profit organization on a mission to combat childhood hunger and promote literacy among children who need it most.

![](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/4ea71a_7343733310474c7f849dae2045f3bf32~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_740,h_576,al_c,q_90,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/4ea71a_7343733310474c7f849dae2045f3bf32~mv2.png)

Led by the compassionate Theresa Monforte-Caraballo, Grandma's Love Inc. has been a pillar of support for families facing food insecurity in New York City. Through their innovative programs, such as Weekend Food Backpacks and Book Vending Machines, the organization has made a significant impact on the lives of countless children.
One of Grandma's Love Inc.'s key initiatives is the Weekend Food Backpack program, which provides children with nutritious meals to take home when school meals are not available. This not only ensures that kids have access to essential nourishment but also alleviates the burden on families struggling to make ends meet.
In addition to tackling hunger, Grandma's Love Inc. is dedicated to promoting literacy and a love for reading among children. The organization's Book Vending Machines, strategically placed in schools in underserved neighborhoods, give kids the opportunity to choose books to keep and cherish. This not only helps improve literacy skills but also fosters a lifelong love of learning.
Through community engagement with schools and families, Grandma's Love Inc. has been able to reach even more children in need. By working closely with educators and community leaders, the organization has been able to tailor its programs to meet the specific needs of each community, ensuring maximum impact and sustainability.
Since its inception, Grandma's Love Inc. has distributed approximately 5,000 bags of groceries to families in need and has touched the lives of countless children in New York City. With a holistic approach that addresses both hunger and literacy, the organization is committed to nurturing the well-being and development of every child it serves.
In a world where the basic needs of so many children go unmet, Grandma's Love Inc. stands as a beacon of hope, proving that a little love and compassion can go a long way in making a difference. Together, we can create a brighter future for all children, one meal and one book at a time.
