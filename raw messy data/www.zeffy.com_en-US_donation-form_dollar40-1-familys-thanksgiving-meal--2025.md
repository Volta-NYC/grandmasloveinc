#### $40 = 1 Family's Thanksgiving Meal

As Thanksgiving approaches, we turn to one of our most heartfelt traditions — our Annual Thanksgiving Food Giveaway.

With your support, we provide complete Thanksgiving meals to the children in our program and their families — families who might otherwise go without a holiday meal.

Your generosity brings more than food to the table — it fills homes with warmth, gratitude, and the joy of Thanksgiving shared together.

**Our goal this season is simple:** ensure every child we serve has a Thanksgiving meal — and a memory — to cherish.

Every $40 donation provides one family with a complete meal, including a turkey and traditional sides. Together, we can make this Thanksgiving a celebration of love, community, and giving.
