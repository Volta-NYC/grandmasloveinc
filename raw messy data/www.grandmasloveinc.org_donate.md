###### Empower Caregivers   with a Moment of Peace

We are raising funds to create thoughtful gift bags for parents and caregivers of children battling cancer or other serious illnesses. Each bag will include a carefully selected collection of items—a spiritual healing book to inspire inner strength, a soothing candle to create a calming atmosphere, an aromatherapy bracelet to promote relaxation throughout the day, and a warm cup accompanied by tea bags or other comforting items—to provide a moment of peace in the midst of daily challenges.

### Donate to make a difference with Grandma's Love, Inc.

At Grandma's Love, Inc., we believe every child deserves the chance to thrive. In underserved communities, many children face hunger and lack access to educational resources. By supporting our mission, you help ensure that children receive nutritious meals and books, fostering their physical, intellectual, and emotional growth.

​

Your financial support helps us provide meals and books to children in need. Every contribution, no matter the size, helps us reach more children and provide essential resources.

Thank you for being a part of our mission!!!
