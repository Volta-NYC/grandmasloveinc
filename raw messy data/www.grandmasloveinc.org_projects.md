# Our Impact

![child being helped to write by an adult](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/41d000_efedb5f6f20d755329ac9d73a84bacbc.jpg/v1/fill/w_160,h_160,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/41d000_efedb5f6f20d755329ac9d73a84bacbc.jpg)

Grandma's Love Inc. has made a significant impact on the well-being and development of children in New York City. Since its inception, the organization has provided approximately 5,000 bags of groceries to families in need and has implemented impactful programs such as Food Distribution Programs, Book Vending Machines, and community engagement with schools in various neighborhoods. The organization's unique selling point lies in its holistic approach to addressing both hunger and literacy, ensuring the well-being and development of children.
